  • Urban Planners & Policy Makers: For evidence-based infrastructure decisions

Why This Matters

As cycling becomes increasingly popular for both commuting and leisure:

  • Understanding accident patterns helps prevent future incidents
  • Identifying high-risk areas guides infrastructure improvements
  • Data-driven insights lead to more effective safety measures
  • Better infrastructure planning can save lives and promote cycling
